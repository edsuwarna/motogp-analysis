ℹ️ About MotoGP Analysis 2026

📊 Dashboard

Championship Top 3 podium, bar chart of points standings (riders/constructors), and recent race results.

🏆 Standings

Full rider and constructor standings tables with category filtering, plus race-by-race points breakdown for the top 10 riders.

🏁 Races

Complete 2026 race calendar with Sprint & Race results. Each session shows rider positions, gaps, best lap times, top speeds, and weather conditions. Circuit info displayed per event. CSV export available per session.

📈 Progression

Season progression multi-line chart showing cumulative points per rider across all rounds. Toggle riders on/off with checkboxes. Includes teammate battle head-to-head comparisons within each constructor.

🏢 Teams

Teams and constructors with rider lineups, points, and constructor colors.

📈 Season Stats

Season statistics: most wins, podiums, fastest laps, and top speed rankings. Rider season progress chart with cumulative points and position tracking. Rider comparison tool — select two riders for a head-to-head stats showdown. Rider Recent Form shows last 5 race results with trend analysis (improving/declining/stable). Rider Season Stats table aggregates wins, podiums, top5s, DNFs across the full season.

🔗 Data Source

Data powered by the official MotoGP pulselive API. Not affiliated with MotoGP or Dorna Sports.

Built with FastAPI, SQLite, Chart.js & TailwindCSS 🏍️
